Rock Paper Scissors

👻 Important Notification 👻

In this spine-chilling yet fun game, you'll face a hungry zombie! It wants to munch on your brain, but out of humanity, it's willing to give you a chance to save yourself. Are you ready for the challenge?

Game Rules:

  1. Rock-Paper-Scissors: You will go head-to-head with the zombie in a game of Rock-Paper-Scissors! Win a round to earn 1 point. Lose or tie, you get nothing.
  2. Score Battle: Your goal is to be the first to reach 5 points! If you can score before the zombie does, it will graciously let you go and leave in peace.
  3. Zombie's Hunger: If the zombie defeats you, your brain becomes its delicious meal! 😱
